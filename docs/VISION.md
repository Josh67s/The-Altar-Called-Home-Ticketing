# Amazing Crew TV Production

## Project

Event Ticketing & Event Management Platform

---

## First Event

THE ALTAR CALLED HOME

---

## Vision

To build a professional event ticketing and event management platform that enables attendees to purchase tickets online, securely pay through Paystack, receive unique QR-code tickets by email, and allows organizers to manage ticket sales, attendee check-in, and event analytics from a centralized dashboard.

---

## Problems We Are Solving

- Manual registration
- Fake payment confirmations
- Paper tickets
- Long queues
- Difficult attendee tracking
- Difficult revenue tracking
- Poor event reporting

---

## Target Users

1. Visitors
2. Administrators
3. Check-in Officers

---

## Success Criteria

- Multiple ticket purchase
- Multiple ticket categories
- QR-code verification
- Automatic email delivery
- Online payment
- Admin dashboard
- Live analytics
- Reusable for future events
