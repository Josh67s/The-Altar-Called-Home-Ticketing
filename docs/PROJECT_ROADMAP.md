## Success Criteria

The platform is considered complete when:

- Users can buy tickets online.
- Payments are verified automatically.
- QR-code tickets are generated.
- Tickets are emailed automatically.
- Check-in officers can scan tickets.
- The admin dashboard displays accurate reports.
- The system is reusable for future Amazing Crew TV Production events.
